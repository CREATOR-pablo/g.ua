Про нас

Vacancy 3: Assembly worker

You will work in a large workshop at a renowned machine builder. The two of you work on a machine that is often sold and assembled outside the Netherlands. You work as a couple on the machines. One colleague is mechanical, the other electrically oriented Workers in mechanical engineering can vary from assembling, frame assembly and assembling machine parts, to connecting, wiring and starting up control systems. Mechanical, electrical and constructive components are everyday life for you. You assemble different types of machines in the high-tech equipped workshop according to the specs and drawings. Together with your direct colleagues, you ensure that these products leave the house in working order and can be installed on location by the commissioners. It is quite an independent position in which you have a lot of variety in your work in a fun and friendly environment.

Requirements:

• Previously worked in technology as a mechanic in mechanical engineering or automotive.

• Experience with electrical engineering, mechanical engineering and/or mechanical engineering.

• A VCA certificate for you and my safety

• At least an MBO 4 diploma.

With a full-time contract you get:

• A gross monthly salary from €2400, depending on your knowledge and experience.

• A permanent contract with the client after 12 months is negotiable.

• 25 well-earned vacation days and associated ADV days.

• Courses and training that suit your field.

• Pension accrual through the STiPP Pension Fund.

• A nice job in technology.

• A travel allowance.

• 8.33% holiday allowance.
